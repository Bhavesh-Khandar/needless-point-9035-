Overview

This winter, take a break from the hustle & bustle of your daily life and soak in the serenity of Mandawa, where time stands still. Tripoto Mindful Retreats have combined the best of a luxury stay along with fun-filled activities that'll make your next getaway a memorable affair. On this luxe cooking retreat, you’ll stay at the 200-year-old Mandawa Kothi and learn how to cook authentic Rajasthani delicacies including Gatte Ki Sabzi and Laal Maas in the royal kitchen!

Holding on to incredible tales from the past, this Kothi was deserted for decades before its owner Rajendra Singh Palawat finally brought it back to life by renovating it in a way that it still holds on to its uniqueness and royalty. And now, you get to experience it first-hand at this Mindful Retreat. Explore Rajasthan differently this time, book your spot at this culinary getaway!

What you will love

  • Unwinding at a heritage haveli that's now a modern boutique hotel
  • Cooking delicious Rajasthani meals with the chef in the royal kitchen
  • Experiencing local traditional activities like 'Tie and Dye' fabric work, and savoring the taste of dal kachori & local sweets

    • What will you experience

    An experiential royal stay

    – A luxurious stay in a heritage Haveli complemented with delectable cuisine

    – Learn to make the exquisite Rajasthani meal in the royal kitchen. (Veg items would include the famous Dahi Baingan, Gatte Ki Sabzi, Lassan ki chutney & non-veg items include Laal Maas, Chicken Jai Singh, etc)

    – Savour the dinner in the beautiful ambiance of the haveli with pretty lighting and a bonfire.


    Cultural exploring

    – Take a tour of hidden gems including fresco-laden streets and local markets, that are perfect for souvenir shopping.

    – A heritage walk with a professional guide to explore the beautiful culture of Shekhawati

    – You can stop by some of the popular local sweet shops and feel the sugar rush! You can also warm up with a cup of chai and dal kachori.

    – Explore the age-old lanes of the region, where the famous 'Tie and Dye' fabrics are made by people who've been doing this for generations


    How to reach

  • By Road: Mandawa Kothi can be reached by road from Delhi (262 km) and Jaipur (170 km).
  • By Train: The nearest railway stations are Churu and Sikar. From there, you can take a cab to reach the Kothi
  • By Air: The nearest airport is Jaipur Airport, 170 km away from the property It takes about 3 hours from the airport to reach Mandawa.
